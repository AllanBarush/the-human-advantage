Por qué ahora

Desde 2020, el entorno se volvió estructuralmente más incierto: pandemia, disrupciones en cadenas de suministro, inflación y endurecimiento monetario. En paralelo, la IA saltó de proyecto a comportamiento cotidiano dentro de las organizaciones. El Work Trend Index 2024 reporta que el 75% de los trabajadores del conocimiento ya usan IA y el 78% traen sus propias herramientas (BYOAI).

Sin embargo, el 70% de las transformaciones fracasan (McKinsey). La ventaja competitiva ya no es tener IA — es tener las capacidades humanas y el sistema de ejecución para liderar con ella. THE HUMAN ADVANTAGE cierra esa brecha en 30 horas con 7 entregables listos para el consejo directivo.

The Program

AI won't replace leaders.
Leaders who understand AI will.

The Human Advantage is an intensive executive program designed for senior leaders navigating the intersection of human judgment and artificial intelligence. Over 30 hours across six modules and a capstone project, participants develop the strategic frameworks needed to lead transformation with clarity, empathy, and measurable impact.

This is not a technology course. It is a leadership accelerator that equips executives with Design Thinking methodology, agile transformation strategies, and a human-centered approach to AI adoption that drives real organizational change.

What You Take Home

Seven executive deliverables.
Zero theory without action.

Every deliverable is built during the program with faculty guidance. You leave with board-ready documents, not just notes.

1
Human-AI Role Map
A comprehensive mapping of human vs. AI responsibilities across your organization's key functions and processes.
2
Agile Transformation Roadmap (90 Days)
A phased, milestone-driven plan for implementing agile practices with clear accountability and success metrics.
3
Executive Storytelling Playbook
Your personal toolkit of narrative frameworks, stakeholder-specific messaging, and change communication strategies.
4
Human Advantage Team Blueprint
A structural design for high-performance teams that optimally integrate human talent with AI capabilities.
5
AI Adoption Plan
A strategic roadmap for responsible AI integration, including vendor evaluation criteria and implementation phases.
6
Risk Control Starter Kit (NIST-Aligned)
A governance framework aligned with NIST AI Risk Management standards, ready for board presentation and compliance review.
7
Impact Scorecard
A measurement framework with KPIs, baselines, and targets to track the ROI of your human-AI transformation initiatives.
